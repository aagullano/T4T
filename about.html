About T4T

Tokens for Tables came from the love of tabletop gaming. T4T want to provide an inexpensive option to those that want to elevate their games. The maker of this website was inspired by all the games that she as a part of over the years and wants to inspire others to start their own games. As a Game Master, having a physical token to illustrate the Player's custom characters, their familiars and campanions, and the monsters they face all change the game. Player imagination is part of the game, but you can give them more.
